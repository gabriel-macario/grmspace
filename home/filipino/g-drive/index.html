Information

By default, the course materials on iLearn are downloaded to whatever device is being used to open them. If you're broke frugal like I am, and have a subpar device for viewing Microsoft Word documents, this can lead to issues while trying to study on the go.

Fortunately, Google Drive is slightly better at viewing these documents, so all iLearn documents have been uploaded to one folder. For organizational purposes, the documents have been itemized. Also, a FIL001 folder containing older documents has been created - in case you need/want to review something.

Additionally, the vocabulary sheets used to make the Quizlets (for both FIL001 and FIL002) can be located in these folders.
