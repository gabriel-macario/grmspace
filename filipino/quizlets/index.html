Information

Once upon a time (Fall 2017), a pretty girl in one of my classes made a quizlet for one of our quizzes and shared it with the class. I have since then fallen in love (and Quizlet is a pretty cool site too).

If you're unfamiliar with Quizlet, it's a extremely flexible flashcard webapp. You make flashcards, and then the site provides several ways to use them. The ones I use the most are the "Learn" function and the "Test" function.

The numbers preceeding each of the quizlets' titles correlate to the itemized iLearn document that provided the information for the quizlet

Note: The original documents on iLearn were not itemized (numbered). However, the copies that have been uploaded to FIL002's Google Drive are.

Still, no matter how great Quizlet is, it's sort of useless if the flashcards are incorrect, and I am nothing if not fallible. So, if you find issues with any of the Quizlets I made (linked below), or if you would like to customize/create your own, the vocabulary sheets can be found within the proper Google Drive Folder.
